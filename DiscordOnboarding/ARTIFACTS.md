##  ARTIFACT SELECTION

### How It Works

1. **Role-Based Recommendations**
   - System recommends artifacts based on your role stack
   - Primary role gets priority recommendation
   - Secondary role gets second recommendation

2. **Choose Your Artifact**
   - Select from 4 recommended artifacts
   - Each artifact unlocks a role
   - Each artifact grants unique perks

3. **Perks Activated**
   - Perks are tracked in user profile
   - Perks provide ongoing benefits
   - Perks can be upgraded over time

##  PERKS SYSTEM

### Perk Types

- **+Daily Discipline** - Bonus points for daily activities
- **+Clarity Drops** - Special clarity insights and guidance
- **+AI Insights** - Enhanced AI assistance and insights
- **+Motivation Bursts** - Random motivation boosts

### Perk Activation

Perks are activated when artifact is selected and stored in user profile. They provide ongoing benefits throughout the user's journey.

---

##  RECOMMENDATION LOGIC

1. **Primary Role** - Gets matching core artifact
2. **Secondary Role** - Gets matching core artifact (if different)
3. **Fill Remaining** - Other core artifacts to reach 4 total

### Example

**Role Stack:** [Vision Builder, Systems Wizard]

**Recommended:**
1. Sword of Progress (Vision Builder)
2. Gem of Genius (Systems Wizard)
3. Compass of Purpose (fill)
4. Torch of Momentum (fill)
