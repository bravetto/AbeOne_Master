üéØ CRITICAL DATA - WHAT TECH TEAM MUST KNOW

WHAT: HIPAA Applies When

  • ‚úÖ B2B Clinical: Selling to providers/payers = Business Associate (HIPAA applies)
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è B2C Wellness: State laws apply, design for HIPAA anyway (future-proof)
  • üîë PHI Definition: Health info + 18 identifiers (names, DOB, SSN, MRN, phone, email, etc.)
  • üîë ePHI: PHI in electronic form (everything your AI sees)

WHY: Core Requirements

  1. Privacy Rule: What you can do with PHI (treatment/payment/operations OK, marketing/research need auth)
  2. Security Rule: How you protect ePHI (encryption, access controls, audit logs)
  3. Breach Notification: When/how to notify (24hrs to CE, 60 days to HHS/individuals)

HOW: Critical Path (Must Have)

Component What Why Critical Who When

Encryption AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit P0 - Required by Security Rule DevOps + Security Week 5-6

WHO: Team Responsibilities

  • Security Officer: Overall HIPAA compliance, risk analysis, incident response
  • Engineering Lead: Technical implementation, security controls, audit logging
  • Legal/Compliance: BAA execution, breach determination, regulatory compliance
  • DevOps: Infrastructure, encryption, monitoring, backups

  • Weeks 3-4: Infrastructure (cloud setup, BAAs executed, integrations secured)

üö® AI-SPECIFIC CRITICAL POINTS

LLM Provider Selection

  • External (OpenAI/Anthropic): ‚úÖ Requires BAA, ‚ö†Ô∏è PHI leaves infrastructure
  • Self-Hosted: ‚úÖ No BAA needed, ‚úÖ PHI stays internal, ‚ö†Ô∏è You own all security
  • Decision: Start external with BAA, consider self-hosted for high-security

Vector Database = ePHI

  • Critical: Embeddings created from PHI ARE ePHI (reversible)
  • Requires: BAA with vector DB vendor (Pinecone/Weaviate) OR self-host
  • Controls: Per-tenant indexes, encryption, audit logging

De-identification = Not PHI

  • Safe Harbor: Remove 18 identifiers (easier, restrictive for free text)
  • Expert Determination: Statistical methods (flexible, requires expert cert)
  • Use Case: Model training, analytics, research (no HIPAA restrictions after de-ID)

AI Incident Scenarios

  1. Prompt injection ‚Üí PHI exfiltration
  2. Mis-routing ‚Üí Wrong patient gets PHI
  3. Log exposure ‚Üí PHI sent to non-BAA vendor
  4. Training leak ‚Üí PHI in model training data
  5. Vector DB breach ‚Üí Unauthorized access to embeddings
