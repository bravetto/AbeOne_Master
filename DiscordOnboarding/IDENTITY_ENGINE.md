##  ROLE ASSIGNMENT RULES

### **Q1: Passions → Archetype**
What fuels you determines your archetype:

-  **Creating** → Creative Warrior
-  **Solving** → Systems Wizard
-  **Connecting** → Influence Architect
-  **Competing** → Quiet Assassin
-  **Building** → Vision Builder / Momentum Beast

### **Q2: Talents → Role**
Your natural talent determines your primary role:

-  **Creativity** → Creative Warrior
-  **Systems Thinking** → Systems Wizard
-  **Leadership** → Influence Architect
-  **Craftsmanship** → Vision Builder
-  **Execution Speed** → Momentum Beast

### **Q3: Vibes → Color**
Your social vibe affects your color/vibe:

-  **Outgoing** → Influence Architect (Gold)
-  **Quiet Assassin** → Quiet Assassin (Gray)
-  **High-Energy** → Momentum Beast (Magenta)
-  **Calm Strategist** → Systems Wizard (Blue)

### **Q4: Intentions → Pathway**
Why you're here determines your pathway:

-  **Level Up** → Vision Builder
-  **Business** → Momentum Beast
-  **Mind Expansion** → Systems Wizard
-  **Tools + Systems** → Systems Wizard
-  **Fun + Chaos** → Creative Warrior

##  ASSIGNMENT LOGIC

### Weight System

1. **Q1 (Archetype):** Weight = 1.0 (Strong)
2. **Q2 (Role):** Weight = 1.0 (Strong)
3. **Q3 (Vibe):** Weight = 0.7 (Medium)
4. **Q4 (Pathway):** Weight = 0.8 (Medium-High)
5. **Q5 (Growth):** Weight = 0.5 (Lower)

### Role Stack Calculation

- Top 3 roles by score
- Minimum score threshold: 0.1 (10%)
- Primary role = highest score
- Secondary roles = next 2 highest scores

##  ROLE STACK

Users can have multiple roles in their stack:

1. **Primary Role** - Highest score (from Q1/Q2)
2. **Secondary Role** - Second highest score
3. **Tertiary Role** - Third highest score

Example:
- Primary: Creative Warrior (45%)
- Secondary: Systems Wizard (30%)
- Tertiary: Influence Architect (25%)
