  5. **How do you want to grow?**
     -  Mastery
     - ‍ Confidence
     -  Creativity
     -  Intelligence
     -  Discipline
     -  Income

### **Step 3: Traits → Role Stack Assignment**
- Personality type revealed
- **Role Stack** unlocked (multiple roles)
- Top 3 traits displayed
- Guardian celebration (Abë)

### **Step 4: Access Pathway → Channels Unlock**
- Channels unlocked based on role stack
- Builder → builders-lounge, projects, showcase
- Thinker → think-tank, strategy, discussions
- Creator → creators-corner, art-gallery, inspiration
- Explorer → explorers-guild, discoveries, adventures
- Guardian → guardians-hall, quality-control, validation

### **Step 5: Artifact Quest → Choose Your Item**
- 6 recommended artifacts shown
- Based on role stack
- Choose your artifact (buttons)
- Artifacts:

  -  Mind Crystal (Thinker)
  -  Brush of Creation (Creator)
  -  Map of Wonders (Explorer)
  -  Shield of Protection (Guardian)
  -  Phoenix Feather (Universal)
  -  Dragon Scale (Universal)

### **Step 6: Personal Profile → Generated by AI**
- AI-generated personal profile
- Based on traits and role stack
- Personalized description
- Role stack displayed
- Top traits shown

### **Step 7: Map Drop → Teleport Buttons**
-  GreatnessZone™ Map shown
- Teleport buttons to:
  -  Home Base
  -  Quest Board
  -  Leaderboard
  -  Profile
  -  Unlocked channels (up to 4)

### **Step 8: First Quest → Immediate Engagement**
- Welcome Quest assigned
- Quest details shown
- Rewards displayed
- "Complete Quest" button
- Immediate engagement
